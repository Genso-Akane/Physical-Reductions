## ⚡ Cause
**3. Energy**

---

## ⚙️ Mechanism
* **`2. Event` → `3. Energy`**
* **`2. Event` → `9. Process`**
* **`7. System` → `8. System State`**
* **`8. System State` + `9. Process` → `12. Entropy`**

Energy defines participation of a system in events.
Process forms a chain of events within the system.
System State defines parameters of changes.
Entropy defines the set of accessible states of the system.

---

## 📡 Effect
* **`3. Energy`** is redistributed within **`9. Process`**.
* **`8. System State`** changes within accessible states.
* **`12. Entropy`** increases as expansion of accessible states.
* **“Thermodynamics”** is description of **`3. Energy`** and **`12. Entropy`** in **`9. Process`** for **`7. System`**.



---

## 🛠 Practical Conclusion
Control of a system is control of energy distribution and accessible states.

**Engineering:**
* control is achieved through control of **`3. Energy`**
* constraints are defined by **`12. Entropy`**
* efficiency is determined by structure of **`9. Process`**
* optimization is implemented through modification of **`8. System State`**
